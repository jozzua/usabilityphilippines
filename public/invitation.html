An Invitation to Celebrate


Dear Visitor

Usability - a hot topic in design. We refer to it as the "ease of use and learnability" of any human made object. It's more than that - it's making design humane, intuitive & emotional.

Want to understand it even more?

UsabilityPhilippines.com, with the Philippine Ergonomics Society, is pleased to invite you to a symposium on World Usability with the theme - EDUCATION: Designing for Social Change. We will focus outwardly on how our organizations, our cultures, and our world impact usability.

We will explore and celebrate:

  • Designing with an intentional outcome of sparking change in how people behave, communicate, and do things in the world; and
  • Examining the concept of cultures and how culture impacts usability.

    • Our Guest Speakers Include:


    Andrei Gonzales

    Communication Designer and Co-founder of Hugo MNL where he practices Identity, Information, and Interactive design.

    Julius Charles Serrano

    Research Director at Even Grounds Accessibility Consulting

    Melissa Limcaoco

    Head of Operations of the New Media Campaign of President Noynoy Aquino.
