World Usability Day Celebration

Theme: Designing for Social Change

December 2, 2011, Waldo Perfecto Seminar Room SPS Building, De La Salle University - Manila 2401 Taft Avenue, Malate, Manila

Event

What: World Usability Day Celebration

When: December 2, 2011

Where:Waldo Perfecto Seminar Room SPS Building, DLSU Manilas


UsabilityPhilippines.com, with the Philippine Ergonomics Society, is pleased to invite you to a symposium on World Usability with the theme - EDUCATION: Designing for Social Change. We will focus outwardly on how our organizations, our cultures, and our world impact usability.

We will explore and celebrate:

  • Designing with an intentional outcome of sparking change in how people behave, communicate, and do things in the world; and
  • Examining the concept of cultures and how culture impacts usability.

    About Usability Philippines

    Usability Philippines is an organization of usability professionals that seeks to advocate the adoption of usability standards in the design of products, systems and interfaces in the Philippines. The organization aims to provide resources for enhancing user experience across platforms as well as collect and disseminate knowledge on usability.

    Our Vision

    We envision Usability Philippines to be recognized by the government and industry as the primary resource for usability knowledge and practice by 2016.
